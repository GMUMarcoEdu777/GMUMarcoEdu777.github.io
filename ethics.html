The guidelines for the Developers

Developers need follow the rules of the ethics that includes Data Privacy and Security is met, there is Accessibility and completeness, have a Compelling Design and User Well being that is keeping user's engaged, Algorithmic Bias by having the data being clean, and lastly Professional Conduct.
